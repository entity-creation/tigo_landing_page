ABOUT US

Tigo Capital Energy Limited is a fully indigenous Nigerian company, incorporated in November 2014, with a strong focus on delivering integrated solutions to the Oil & Gas, Marine, and Energy sectors. Leveraging extensive industry expertise, we provide end-to-end procurement and supply chain solutions, supported by capabilities in land and marine logistics, equipment leasing, asset integrity management, construction logistics, HVAC system packages, and project management/consultancy services. Our operations are driven by a commitment to excellence, safety, and efficiency, ensuring the timely and cost-effective delivery of projects in line with global industry standards. Amidst other services, Tigo Capital Energy ltd offers a comprehensive offshore marine logistics services focusing on providing all types of offshore equipment ranging from AHTS, FSIV, PSV, JUB, TUGS, LHT, Security patrol boat and personalized and specialized services to meet each client’s specific needs. Heat Exchanger Services provides comprehensive solutions for the maintenance and repair of heat exchangers. Our experienced team of professionals offers a wide range of services, including Heat exchanger services involve the maintenance, repair, cleaning, and optimization of heat exchangers to ensure efficient heat transfer in various industrial applications.

OUR VISION 

To be a leading indigenous energy services company in Nigeria and across Africa, delivering world-class procurement, logistics, and engineering support solutions that consistently exceed client expectations. Tigo Capital energy will be dynamic and analytical in responding to client problems and offer solid solutions. We will provide quality technical support/services to our numerous clients in the industry and give them value for their money even at a reasonable discount in the present area of operations. We shall not relent in the training of our employee up to date knowing fully well that our success will depend on them. Above all, we carry out activities in such manner as not to infringe on the environment or violate local regulations that will have impacts on the image of both the service provider and the client.

OUR MISSION

To provide integrated, end-to-end procurement, supply chain, and logistics solutions to the Oil & Gas, Marine, and Energy sectors by leveraging technical expertise, innovative practices, and a highly skilled workforce, while ensuring safety, quality, efficiency, and sustainability in all our operations.
