ABOUT US




Tigo Capital Energy Limited is a fully indigenous Nigerian company incorporated in November 2014. The firm specializes in delivering integrated, high-impact solutions across the Oil & Gas, Marine, and Energy sectors.










OUR VISION
 

To be a leading indigenous energy services company in Nigeria and across Africa, delivering world-class procurement, logistics, and engineering support solutions that consistently exceed client expectations. Tigo Capital energy will be dynamic and analytical in responding to client problems and offer solid solutions. We will provide quality technical support/services to our numerous clients in the industry and give them value for their money. Above all, we carry out activities in such manner as not to infringe on the environment or violate local regulations that will have impacts on the image of both the service provider and the client.

OUR MISSION

To provide integrated, end-to-end procurement, supply chain, and logistics solutions to the Oil & Gas, Marine, and Energy sectors by leveraging technical expertise, innovative practices, and a highly skilled workforce, while ensuring safety, quality, efficiency, and sustainability in all our operations.

OUR CORE VALUES

  • Safety. We prioritize the health, safety, and security of our people, clients, and the environment by maintaining a zero-harm culture in all operations. 
  • Excellence. We are committed to delivering high-quality services and continuously improving our processes to meet and exceed industry standards
  • Integrity. We conduct our business with honesty, transparency, and strong ethical standards, building trust with clients and stakeholders.
  • Reliability. We deliver on our promises “on time, within scope, and with consistency”, ensuring dependable service delivery at all times.
  • Innovation. We embrace technology and creative solutions to improve efficiency and provide value-driven services to our clients.
  • Customer Focus. We place our clients at the center of everything we do, tailoring solutions to meet their specific needs and expectations.
  • Sustainability. We operate responsibly, minimizing environmental impact and contributing positively to the communities in which we work.

OUR TEAM

We are a multidisciplinary team of skilled and experienced professionals dedicated to delivering prompt, efficient, and client-focused solutions. Backed by proven engineering and business management practices, we provide high-quality services in marine logistics, supply chain management, and asset integrity.

Our commitment to international quality standards, operational excellence, and customer satisfaction ensures that we consistently deliver reliable services and exceptional value. With a talented and versatile workforce, we pride ourselves on professionalism, competence, and performance-driven results
