Health, Safety, Security & Environment (HSSE)

Tigo Capital Energy Limited is committed to conducting all its operations in a manner that protects the health and safety of its employees, contractors, clients, host communities, and the environment, while safeguarding assets and ensuring business continuity. Our policy is aligned with the HSSE requirements and expectations of our clients, including Chevron, Shell, and NNPC Limited, as well as applicable Nigerian regulations and international standards

OUR COMMITMENTS
 

TIGO Capital Energy Limited shall:

• Ensure full compliance with all applicable laws and regulations of the Nigerian Upstream Petroleum Regulatory Commission and National Environmental Standards and Regulations Enforcement Agency.
• Adopt and implement HSSE Management Systems consistent with international standards (ISO 45001 & ISO 14001).
• Pursue a Goal Zero philosophy which is “no harm to people, no damage to the environment, and no loss of assets”.

• Identify hazards, assess risks, and implement effective control measures for all operations.
• Prevent incidents, injuries, occupational illnesses, and environmental pollution.
• Promote a strong HSSE culture driven by leadership, accountability, and employee participation.
• Ensure all personnel are competent, trained, and medically fit for assigned tasks.
• Provide and enforce the use of appropriate Personal Protective Equipment (PPE).
• Maintain effective emergency preparedness and response systems.
• Ensure safe design, procurement, operation, and maintenance of facilities and equipment.
• Engage and protect host communities while respecting environmental and social sustainability.
• Continuously monitor, audit, and improve HSSE performance and policy in accordance to approved standard. 

ENVIRONMENTAL STEWARDSHIP



TIGO Capital Energy Limited shall:
• Minimize environmental footprint by reducing emissions, waste, and discharges
• Prevent oil spills and ensure prompt response and remediation
• Promote efficient use of energy and natural resources
• Protect biodiversity and ecosystems within operational areas.

SECURITY POLICY

The security of all employees, equipment and property is of utmost importance to us. Staff movement log books are kept on all our sites and updated regularly to check worker movement and ensure their safety and security. All employees are provided with company identity cards and the clients own, where necessary. Trained staffs are on guard twenty four hours in all work sites.

RESPONSIBILITIES

• Management: Provide leadership, resources, and enforce HSSE compliance.
• Supervisors: Ensure safe work practices and enforce procedures.
• Employees & Contractors: Comply with HSSE rules, use PPE, and report hazards.
• HSSE Department: Monitor compliance, conduct audits, and drive continuous improvement.
